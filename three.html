Hospitality Management & Tourism

Welcome

The School of Hospitality Management and Tourism, Cathal Brugha Street, has been synonymous with the hospitality and catering industry in Ireland since 1941. In Ireland, this School is the leading centre of learning and teaching in hospitality, tourism, event and leisure management.

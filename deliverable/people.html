Background

Dr. Jennifer Lawlor is a lecturer in Management, Services Management and Strategic Management at undergraduate and Mastersâ€™ level in the School of Hospitality Management and Tourism, Faculty of Tourism and Food, Dublin Institute of Technology. She was awarded a PhD in the Faculty of Business and Management at the University of Ulster in 2008.

Dr. Lawlor has presented a number of papers at national and European conferences in the area of inter-firm collaboration and co-operation. She also authored a book chapter on Trends and Developments in the Hospitality Sector. She has acted as an academic reviewer of competitive papers for the annual British Academy of Management (BAM) Conference.

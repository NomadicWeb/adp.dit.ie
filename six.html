The International Office promotes DIT internationally and establishes international
links and partnerships with a range of institutions globally. We recruit international
students from over 70 countries and ensure their support before and during their time at DIT.

We represent DIT at education exhibitions and events in a wide number of
regions meeting potential applicants, managing overseas agents and welcoming
international delegations from all over the world. Subscribe to our blog for news
and updates and to see when we will next be in your country
